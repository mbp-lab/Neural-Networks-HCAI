# Introduction to Neural Networks 🧠

This repository contains the weekly assignments for the course **"Introduction to Neural Networks"**.  
All assignments are provided as Jupyter Notebooks and are designed to help you build both theoretical understanding and practical skills in neural networks. 

You can complete the assignments **either locally** on your own computer or **in Google Colab**. For Google Colab, open the notebook links provided in Moodle, or Upload the notebook to Google Drive and open it in Colab.

## 🧾 Submission Guidelines

- File naming convention: Use the following pattern for all submissions:

```Groupname_AssignmentName.ipynb```

- Ensure all cells are executed and outputs are visible.

- Upload your completed notebook to Moodle.

- Colab users: Download your finished notebook (.ipynb) from Colab before submission.

- Late submissions: Notebooks submitted after the deadline may not be graded unless prior arrangements have been made.
